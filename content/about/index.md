Hello! I'm **Lucas Li**, or as I'm known online, **Gelzone Unsas**. I’m a system engineer, game developer, and music producer who enjoys exploring the intersection of technology, creativity, and personal expression.

{{< float-image src="/images/profile/IMG_5865.JPG" alt="Lucas" max_width="300px" >}}

I’ve always been drawn to the creative freedom of game development and programming. It allows me to combine my technical background from my undergraduate and master’s studies in Computer Science with my passion for storytelling and music. Through my work, I focus on building experiences that blend [music production](https://www.youtube.com/@GelzoneUnsas) and [programming](https://github.com/GelzoneXUnsas), whether that’s experimenting with interactive systems, prototyping new ideas, or developing immersive digital environments.

Outside of coding, I’m a fingerstyle guitar enthusiast and a huge fan of anime. The emotional depth in music and the storytelling in anime continue to shape how I think about design, pacing, and atmosphere. I’m especially interested in creating experiences that feel personal and emotionally resonant, not just technically impressive.

Thanks for stopping by, and feel free to reach out if you’d like to connect!
